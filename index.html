Types of-Debts We Deal With

We help with a wide range of debt problems. Below are the most common types we support.

CouncilTax

Arrears with your local authority can quickly lead to bailiff involvement. We help you set up affordable payment plans and negotiate with councils to stop enforcement.

CreditCards

Unsecured credit card debt is one of the most common reasons for county judgments (CCJs) and bailiff visits. We can advise on repayment options and debt solutions.

Catalogues

Mail order and cataloguedebts, if left unpaid, may be passed to enforcement agents. We help you understand your rights and find a way forward.

Parking & Traffic Fines

Penaltycharge notices (PCNs) and unpaid parking tickets can escalate to bailiff action. We explain the process and how to challenge or settle them.

HMRC & Tax-Debts

Outstanding tax, VAT, or self-assessment balances can result in HMRC using bailiffs. We provide guidance on time-to-pay arrangements and professional advice.

ChildMaintenance

The ChildMaintenance Service can instruct enforcement agents for arrears. We help you understand your options and protect your family.

Fines

Unpaid magistrate-s' courtfines are among thedebts with the strongest-enforcement powers. We advise on how to deal with fines and avoid further action.

Rent-Arrears

Private or social housing rent arrears may lead to eviction and bailiff involvement. We explain your rights and how to negotiate with landlords.
