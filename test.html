Understanding debt solutions

Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)

England, Wales, NI

IVA Advantages

  • One affordable monthly payment – living costs taken into account
  • Stop interest and charges (frozen by creditors)
  • Write off significant % of debt after ~5 years
  • Protect your home and vehicle (reasonable value)
  • Stop worrying calls & letters – legally binding
  • Stop court and bailiff action

IVA Disadvantages

  • Requires creditor agreement (75% in value)
  • Keeping to a strict budget can be difficult
  • Affects credit score for 6 years
  • Added to Individual Insolvency Register (removed 3 mo. after)
  • Fees are included in your monthly payment
  • Need approval for credit over £500

About IVA – how it works

An Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA) is a legally binding agreement to pay back an affordable portion of debt, usually over 5 years. An Insolvency Practitioner prepares a proposal; creditors vote. If 75% agree, the IVA is approved. From your monthly payment, fees are deducted. After successful completion, remaining unsecured debt is written off.

Debt Relief Order (DRO)

England & Wales only

DRO Benefits

  • Debt-free after 12 months
  • No monthly repayments
  • 100% of unsecured debt written off
  • Legal protection from creditors

DRO Disadvantages

  • Not available to home owners
  • Added to Individual Insolvency Register
  • Affects credit score 6 years
  • Strict criteria: disposable income ≤£75, assets ≤£2k, vehicle ≤£4k, debt ≤£50k

How DRO works

Lasts 12 months, no payments. Apply via an Approved Intermediary. Must meet low income/asset criteria. After 12 months, debts written off.

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

informal

DMP Advantages

  • One affordable monthly repayment
  • No need to deal directly with creditors
  • Interest & charges may be frozen (not guaranteed)
  • Flexible, informal, good for short-term changes

DMP Disadvantages

  • No debt forgiveness – usually full repayment
  • Length not fixed, can be long term
  • Credit score affected
  • No legal protection – creditors may still take action

About DMP

Informal agreement via a debt management company. They calculate your disposable income and distribute payments pro-rata. May involve setup/ monthly fees (charities offer free plans).

Bankruptcy

England & Wales

Bankruptcy Advantages

  • Debt free after ~12 months (discharged)
  • Legal protection – creditors stop
  • Available to everyone (no strict criteria)
  • Interest frozen

Bankruptcy disadvantages

  • Lose control of assets (home, valuable possessions)
  • £680 fee (may be instalments) before application
  • May need to pay from income for up to 3 years
  • Credit file affected 6 years, on public register
  • May affect employment / being a director

Bankruptcy explained

Formal procedure. Apply via gov.uk with £680 fee. Official Receiver manages assets. Usually discharged after 12 months, but restrictions may apply. Available in England and Wales.

Your credit rating may be affected. Fees apply on successful applications (where applicable) and will be advised before you commit. IVA only in England, Wales, NI – DRO / Bankruptcy England & Wales only.
